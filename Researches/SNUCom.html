SNUComm

SNUComm activity photo

Through Seoul National University's center-based education, SNUComm, we utilize human and material infrastructure based on the Seoul National University Education Cooperation Center to support educational programs for students, parents, and local residents in Siheung City, contributing to the development of a community learning culture in the local society.

In the 'Astro Club', we not only aim to enhance understanding of Earth and the celestial bodies surrounding it, but also provide opportunities to discover the principles of science hidden or overlooked in daily life, making the subject of science more approachable.

By connecting with the elementary school curriculum, the club offers celestial observation activities that are difficult to conduct in schools, helping students better comprehend classroom lessons. In addtion, by experiencing the actual research process of scientists, students can develop scientific thinking and cultivate basic scientific literacy.
