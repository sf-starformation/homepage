Ph.D. Ilseung Han Begins His Research Journey in Spain

  • Hanju Nam
  • Jan 10, 2025

Our team member Ilseung Han obtained Ph.D. from University of Science and Technology in August, 2024, supervised by Woojin Kwon. His dissertation title is “A Comprehensive Study of Grain Growth in Class I Young Stellar Objects”. He moved to Barcelona, Spain, and joined ICE-CSIC as a postdoctoral researcher.
